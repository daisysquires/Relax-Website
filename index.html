Meditation
Meditation can be a great tool to remove all the negative thoughts and clear you mind. It can help to control your breathing and bring a sense of peace back into your life. Find a quiet place and meditate for as long as you feel comfortable.
Music
Make a playlist of songs that always get you in a chilled out mood. Then whenever you need, you've got a great playlist to kick back to. Apps like spotify have premade playlists if you are struggling to create one yourself!
Friends
Hanging out with friends is a great way to relax. You could go and get some ice-cream or take a walk together. The right company can always put you in a better mood, even if it's just over the phone.
Nature
Spending time in nature can allow us to deconnect from life's stressors. There are proven benefits to our mental health, cognitive ability and emotional well-being when we spend more time in nature. Even a quick walk outside might prove very relaxing.
Make your favourite dish
Cooking up your favourite dish is not only a great way to relax, you also get to eat your favourite food after! That sounds like a win win. Cooking can be very therapeutic and the smells will be heavenly.
Physical activity
Excercise can be a great tool to relax. Get the oxygen flowing around your body with some gentle yoga, or take a light jog outside and breathe in the fresh air.
